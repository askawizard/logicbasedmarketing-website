Frequently Asked Questions

What's the guarantee on the Diagnostic?
Simple: If we don't identify at least $500 worth of actionable insights during your 2-hour session, we'll refund your fee entirely. We've never had to issue a refund. This should tell you something about how thorough we are.
What data do I need to provide?
The last 12 months of data from your accounting system (QuickBooks), e-commerce or CRM platform, ad accounts, email platform, and Google Analytics. We provide a detailed guide with step-by-step instructions for each system. Most clients spend 45 minutes to 1 hour gathering everything.
How long until I see results from the retainer?
Quick wins often show up in week one (like finding unused software subscriptions). Revenue-impacting changes usually show results within 30–60 days. The best results compound over 3–6 months as we systematically optimize each channel.
What if we're already working with another agency?
We work well alongside other agencies. The Diagnostic is designed to give you clarity on what's working and what's not across your entire business. Sometimes that means staying the course; sometimes it means making changes. Either way, you'll have data-backed recommendations.
