Frequently Asked Questions

What happens to my $500 after the Challenge?
If we find $50K+ in recoverable losses, your $500 becomes store credit toward any package. If we don't find $50K+, you get a full cash refund. The only requirements are that you provide the requested data exports and attend the findings session.
What data do I need to provide?
The last 12 months of data from your accounting system (QuickBooks), e-commerce or CRM platform, ad accounts, email platform, and Google Analytics. I provide a detailed guide with step-by-step export instructions. Most people spend 45 minutes to 1 hour gathering everything.
What's the difference between The Report and The Fix?
The Report tells you what's broken and how to fix it. The Fix means I do it for you. If you have a team that can execute, The Report is enough. If you want someone to actually implement the changes, renegotiate vendors, and rebuild your tracking — that's The Fix.
Do I need a long-term commitment?
No. The Challenge is one session. The Report and The Fix are one-time engagements with defined deliverables. Embedded CRO is quarterly. Nothing auto-renews.
