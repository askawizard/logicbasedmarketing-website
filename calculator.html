Question 03
Do you know which of your marketing channels is actually profitable, not just which ones bring in revenue?
Revenue and profit are different numbers. A channel can drive a lot of orders and still lose you money once you factor in real costs. Most businesses don't know the difference until it's too late.
Question 04
In the last 12 months, has your business increased marketing spend without seeing proportional revenue growth?
This is the clearest signal of a data problem: spending more, growing less. It almost always means money is going to channels that look good on paper but don't actually perform.
Question 05
Do you know your real customer acquisition cost?
Ad platforms show ROAS. That tells you revenue relative to ad spend. It doesn't tell you what it actually costs to get a customer when you count payroll, software, fulfillment, ops, and returns. Most businesses are 5x to 15x off on this number.
Question 06

AI tools produce garbage outputs when fed garbage data. If your systems don't talk to each other, AI makes things worse, not better.
